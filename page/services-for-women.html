CORE for Women

It is important that women aspiring to study on the CORE programme read all the general information about the CORE philosophy and flexible study system—all of this information is relevant to both men and women.
 
On that note, there are some modules that we currently may not be able to facilitate for female students—on-campus, online or both. It is for this reason that we advise sisters to take advantage of modules that are currently available to them, as we are working on finding alternatives for women.
 

Modules Currently Available for Women

Currently, there are no classes available to women.
 

Online Streaming User Experience

We are aware of some complaints and grievances experienced by some of our users with this service and would like to apologise for the inconveniences. To help us optimise this service in the future, please do the following:
 
1. Technical issues: Report any technical issues to info@daralarqam.co.uk
2. Questions: Click the Dar Al-Arqam logo on the media player. It will direct you to our online page where questions can be submitted.
3. Long silences: If there is a sudden silence, check to see whether it is time for prayer. If it is, then this is most probably the reason for the idle status. It usually takes around 15-20 minutes for classes to resume after prayers.
4. Delays to start-time: You teacher may have been delayed. Please see our online page for any notifications and check back after 15-20 minutes.
5. Cancellations: Be sure to sign up to our WhatsApp broadcast that sends out the most up-to-date course information.
