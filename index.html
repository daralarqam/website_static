thecoreprogramme

Dar Al-Arqam's flagship program for Muslim men and women rethinks leadership thought and practice.

The CORE Programme combines 3 fundamental strands: Development, Islamic Education & Skills.


Objective

Its objective is to produce conscientious leaders who not only understand the specific needs of people they are responsible for, but also have the abilities, knowledge and skillset to cater for them.

The programme also fulfils a Muslim's religious obligation (Far 'Ain) to learn certain things about their religion.

Free for everyone

At Dar Al-Arqam we believe that empowerment is the right of every Muslim, that is why the CORE is completely free.
