Course Overview

A new module for adults that experience difficulty in reciting the Qur’an. The main learning outcomes are (1) the proper recitation of the Quran and (2) confidence in reciting it.
Students can expect:

- Correct pronunciation of Arabic letters
- Basic tajwid rules (vocal embellishment)
- Memorisation of short chapters from the end of the Quran
- Repeated in-classroom practice

The course assumes students can already recognise and read Arabic letters, but cannot recite the Quran correctly or do so with great difficulty and therefore require practice under expert tutelage.

This is a two-part course. Once students have completed the first part they will qualify for the second part which will seek to strengthen and enhance their recitation and relationship with the Quran.
