Course Overview

As part of the CORE curriculum, we are pleased to announce another Skills workshop:

Effective Speaking is a workshop on speaking effectively in a public context.

What makes public speaking different than, say, just talking to a crowd of people, is in the way information is conveyed. In public speaking, the information is purposeful and meant to inform, influence or entertain a group of listeners. Oral presentations at university or during a meeting at work are simple examples of speaking directly to a live audience.

This workshop will cover a number of key areas in public speaking including:

  • Using body language and understanding its impact
  • Effective use of voice and intonation
  • Writing speeches
  • Analysing an orator delivering a speech
